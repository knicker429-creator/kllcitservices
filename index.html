Why is my WiFi slow even though I pay for gigabit speeds?

The most common cause is your router — not your internet plan. Incorrect channel settings, poor router placement, outdated firmware, and band steering errors can cut your actual device speeds in half or worse. KLLC IT Services diagnoses the exact cause and fixes it. Summerlin homes on Cox gigabit regularly achieve 900–1,200 Mbps after a proper network configuration.

Do you fix WiFi dead zones in large Summerlin homes?

Yes — dead zone elimination is a core KLLC IT Services specialty. Large Summerlin homes require proper mesh network configuration, router placement strategy, and channel optimization. Most dead zone problems are solved without purchasing new equipment.

Can you set up Ring, Nest, Alexa, and smart home devices?

Yes — smart home integration is a core service. KLLC IT Services configures Ring doorbells and cameras, Nest thermostats, Amazon Alexa, Sonos audio systems, and other IoT devices. Most smart home problems are network configuration issues — devices dropping offline, failing to respond, or conflicting with each other — which are diagnosed and resolved as part of the setup.

Is my home network secure?

Most residential networks have at least one significant vulnerability — outdated router firmware, default manufacturer credentials, weak DNS settings, or no firewall rules. KLLC IT Services performs a comprehensive network security assessment covering router configuration, DNS filtering, firewall setup, and VPN options. You receive a plain-English report with a prioritized remediation plan.
